VIBASS 9 is the ninth edition of the summer school on Bayesian Statistics to be held from 6th to 9th July 2026 in València (Spain).
Here is a small [history of VIBASS and its past editions]({{ site.url }}/assets/vibass9/VIBASS_history.pdf).

The four days of VIBASS are organised in __two parts__:

1. The first two days include a [__basic course on Bayesian learning__]({{ site.url }}/events/vibass9-intro/) (12 hours), alternating conceptual and practical sessions with R packages.

	It would be advisable that participants bring their laptops for the practical sessions.

2. The [second part]({{ site.url }}/events/vibass9-course/) is dedicated to an __issue of current interest in Bayesian research__. 

	This edition is dedicated to __Bayesian survival, longitudinal and joint models with INLA__.

	This [12 h course]({{ site.url }}/events/vibass9-course/) is provided by [__Denis Rustand__](https://github.com/DenisRustand), Research Scientist at the Biostatistics team, Bordeaux Population Health Research Center, National Institute of Health and Medical Research (Inserm UMR 1219), Bordeaux, France.
  
  [![image]({{ site.urlimg }}denis-rustand.jpg)]({{ site.url }}/events/vibass9-course/)
